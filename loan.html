Top Personal Online Loans In The Philippines

In recent years, there has been an increase in the demand for loan online apps in the Philippines. While there are so many online loan providers, selecting the perfect platform can be daunting, especially for users new to the online loan industry.

To help out, we will explore some of the best personal online loan providers you can find in the Philippines and what they offer.

The Best Online Loans Apps In The Philippines

Below are some of the best online loan apps you will find in the Philippines:

Cash-Express
Loan Amount:
1,000-20,000 PHP
Cash-Express is one of the top online personal loan providers in the Philippines. Like most online loans, you receive your loan money directly to your bank account, and the overall application process is easy. Once you create a profile and submit your application, you will receive a decision within 15 minutes.
One of the unique highlights of Cash-Express is the ability to extend repayment dates with provable causes of delay in payment. This online loan gives Philippine users between 1,000 and 20,000 PHP, with repayment plans of around 7 to 14 days for low-interest repayments.
Finbro.ph
Loan Amount:
1,000-50,000 PHP
Finbro.ph was founded in 2006 and is a quick money lending platform designed for Filipino users. The online loan service is intended for Philippines residents between 20 to 65 years old. Like most other top online personal loan services, Finbro.ph only requires one valid ID and a selfie for users to complete loan applications. Repayment plans can also reach 12 months, making the overall usage experience worthwhile for most Filipino users.
Moneycat
Loan Amount:
500-20,000 PHP
Though Moneycat is an online lending platform, it is designed for Filipino working professionals who earn salaries. As a Filipino working and residing in the Philippines, you can apply for this loan and receive between 500 to 20,000 depending on your credit ranking. The loan is designed only for individuals between the ages of 22 and 60, with a monthly interest of 11.9% on your total loan amount.
Digido
Loan Amount:
1,000-25,000 PHP
Digido is yet another popular online personal loan service available to Filipino users. Like Cash-Express, Digido offers personal online loans of up to 25,000 PHP and a long-term repayment plan fixed between 3-6 months. This platform gives first-time users zero-interest loans when applying for quick 7-day loans.
The loan service is eligible for all Filipino residents between 21-70 years of age. The loan application process only takes about 4 minutes, and there is no need for a guarantor.
